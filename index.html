About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of California, Riverside in the Physics and Astronomy Department, working with Dr. Naveen Reddy on the baryon cycle. My research interest is galaxy formation and evolution, especially the connection between galaxies and the circumgalactic medium (CGM) and intergalactic medium (IGM). I study galactic-scale outflows and inflows of high-redshift star-forming galaxies using low-ionization interstellar metal absorption lines. My work uses high- and low-resolution spectroscopy of galaxies in the MOSDEF survey. Please visit my Research page for more information.
